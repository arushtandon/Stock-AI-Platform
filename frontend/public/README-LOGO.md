# Safron logo

The header shows a **logo mark** (icon) plus the word "SAFRON". To use your own logo:

1. Replace `safron-logo.svg` in this folder with your company logo file.
2. Keep the filename `safron-logo.svg` so the app picks it up everywhere, **or** use a PNG (e.g. `safron-logo.png`) and update all `src="/safron-logo.svg"` in the app to `src="/safron-logo.png"`.
3. Recommended: SVG or PNG, with an icon/mark on the left and optional wordmark. Approx. 220×48px viewBox or equivalent ratio.

The current SVG is a placeholder emblem (diamond shape). Swap in your real logo for the correct branding.
